Creamy Spinach Chicken

Description

A nice quick dish -- there's never enough spinach! :-)

You can serve this dish with gnocci or with any pasta.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breasts
  • 500g of frozen spinach leaves
  • 400ml of cream
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Put olive oil to a pan. Add chopped onion, and after a while add garlic.
  2. Add chicken breasts along with salt and pepper and roast them in the pan.
  3. Add spinach and let it unfreeze.
  4. Add cream and boil for about 15 minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper as needed.
  6. Serve with gnocci or any pasta.
