Creamy Chicken Curry with Pasta

Description

One of my favorite quick recipes. Chicken with curry and pasta

Easy but delicious!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of chicken breasts
  • 400 ml of cream
  • a spoon of curry
  • a spoon of soy sauce
  • one big onion
  • salt
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Put olive oil in a pan and add chopped onion.
  2. Add chicken meat with some salt, pepper, and a spoon of soy souce
  3. After a couple of minutes, add cream and curry.
  4. Cook for about 15 minutes. Add salt and pepper if needed.
  5. Serve with pasta.
